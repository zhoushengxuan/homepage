I am a CIMI postdoc in Institut de Mathématiques de Toulouse, working under the mentorship of Prof. Henri Guenancia. Before this, I obtained my PhD in July 2024 at Peking University under the supervision of Prof. Gang Tian .

My research interest lies in Kähler Geometry, Riemannian Geometry and related topics. Here is my CV.
